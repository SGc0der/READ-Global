About READ Bhutan- Low Literacy

Bhutan is one on a low rank on illiteracy ranking of Asia. Before READ established their first Center there, Bhutan only had ONE public lending library in the whole country. Bhutan was kept stranger to the modern world till the 1960s.

READ Global is one of the only international non-profit organisations in Bhutan as of today.



Core Programs

Some of our most popular trainings in Bhutan include:

  • Literacy Programs- In Collaboration with Ministry of Education

  • Reading and writing groups

  • Computer literacy courses and classes

  • Women's health lectures and classes

  • Community radio

  • Livelihood skills trainings for women home-based workers

  • Small scale businesses' skills and management

  • Women's leadership and civic contribution
