READ India currently is active over 16 Indian states.




Since 2007, READ India has been playing a major role in developing community engagement, empowerment, and education through a well-structured approach. READ India enriches the calibre of local communities to run their independent enterprises or secure suitable jobs for sustaining their economic status.

The cumulative READ India Effect has resulted in spreading of knowledge and skill, prospering of communities and better prospects for our nigh future.

READ India is an independent, FCRA-registered Indian not-for-profit NGO enhancing the disadvantaged communities of India, since 2007 24/7. At READ India, we believe in 'Community First'.



Core Programs

Education Milestones:

READ India's educational milestones focusses on developing habits for reading and writing regularly, while it also offers top courses for English, both oral and writing skills, digital learning for the upcoming generation of computers, coaching and early valuable childhood education.

ICT Training:

Providing access to Information and Communication Technology(ICT) tools and training on basic and advanced use of technology, particularly with engagement in mind. A true necessity for the future.

Women Empowerment and Livelihood:

READ India focusses on empowering women by building their skills, capacities, calibre and creating an enabling environment with stakeholders committed to their socio-economic development with special focus on confidence building, financial literacy and leadership qualities and values.

Youth Skilling and Calibre Enrichment:

READ India erects the capacity of youth in the age group 18 to 25 years through life-skills, career counselling, personality development workshops and courses, English communication and extra-curricular activities such as sports for development.

Health Consciousness and Awareness:

Besides constructing safe and hygenic toilets in schools, READ India also conducts workshops on basic health and hygiene, trains community health workers, and holds events on reproductive and child health at READ Centres.
